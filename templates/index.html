About Our Project

Our project is designed to revolutionize the agricultural value chain by creating a seamless connection between farmers and dealers. We provide two distinct interfaces: one for users (farmers) and another for dealers.

Farmers can easily view and compare live market prices offered by various dealers. This transparency helps farmers make informed decisions about where to sell their crops, ensuring they get the best possible price.

Dealers, on the other hand, can manage their pricing strategies and reach a wider network of farmers. This not only simplifies the process of acquiring crops but also allows dealers to maintain competitive pricing and better manage their inventory.

Our solution aims to enhance efficiency, transparency, and fairness in the agricultural market, benefiting all stakeholders involved.
